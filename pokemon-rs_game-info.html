Pokémon Ruby and Sapphire

Game Information

Alternate Name:
  • ポケットモンスタールビー Pocket Monsters Ruby
  • ポケットモンスターサファイア Pocket Monsters Sapphire
Category: RPG
Developer: Game Freak
Publisher: Nintendo
Release Dates:
  • Nov. 21, 2002 [Japan]
  • Mar. 19, 2003 [North America]
  • Apr. 3, 2003 [Australia]
  • Jul. 25, 2003 [Europe]

Pokémon Ruby Version and Pokémon Sapphire Version are the third installments of the Pokémon series of role-playing video games. They are the first paired core series of Generation III games that are set in the Hoenn region.
